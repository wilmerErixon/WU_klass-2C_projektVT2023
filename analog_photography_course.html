What is analog photography?

Analog photography, also known as film photography, is the process of capturing images on photographic film. This method of photography was the primary way of taking photographs for over a century, until digital photography became popular in the early 2000s.

Analog cameras use film rolls that are loaded into the camera and exposed to light to create a negative image. The negatives are then developed in a darkroom, where the image is enlarged and printed onto photographic paper.

Analog photography has a unique, vintage aesthetic that has remained popular even in the age of digital photography. It requires a different set of skills and techniques compared to digital photography, and is enjoyed by many as a form of artistic expression.

About the course

An analog photography course typically includes a variety of topics related to film photography. Participants in the course can expect to learn about the basics of analog cameras, including camera types, lenses, and film formats. The course will also cover camera settings such as aperture, shutter speed, and ISO.

In addition to learning about cameras and settings, participants will also learn about film processing and darkroom techniques. This includes developing film, printing photographs, and various techniques for manipulating images in the darkroom. Participants will also learn about the various types of film available and how to choose the right film for different types of photography.

Most analog photography courses also cover topics such as composition, lighting, and creative techniques for making unique and interesting photographs. Participants will learn about different types of photography such as portraiture, landscape, and still life photography.

Throughout the course, participants will have opportunities to practice their skills by taking photographs and developing their own film. Instructors will provide feedback and guidance to help participants improve their technique and develop their own style.

Overall, an analog photography course is a comprehensive introduction to the world of film photography. It is designed for beginners who are interested in learning the basics of film photography and developing their own unique style.
